vehicle to vehicle communication usign LI-FI technology

Vehicle-to-vehicle communication has proven to be the most successful method of reducing vehicle accidents. In this study, the suggested application of Li-Fi technology consists primarily of light-emitting diode bulbs as a means of connectivity, with data sent over the spectrum of light as an optical wireless medium for signal propagation.
